ABOUT THE PROJECT

What we're building

Sydstevns Energifællesskab is a 2.5 MW solar project in Sydstevns, fully owned by the people who live here.

When it generates electricity, co-owners receive a share of the revenue. It's a straightforward way to support clean energy while earning returns — and keeping those benefits local.

2.5 MW capacity
1,250 homes powered annually
850 tons CO₂ avoided each year
25 years expected project life
CO-OWNERSHIP

What you get as a co-owner

Co-ownership is more than symbolic. Here's what it means in practice:

Annual returns

Each share earns projected returns of 5-7% per year. Payments distributed annually.

Lower energy rates

Co-owners can access discounted energy directly from the project.

Voting rights

One member, one vote. You have a say in how the project is run.

Full transparency

Quarterly updates on performance, finances, and impact. No guesswork.
